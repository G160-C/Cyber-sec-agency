# Security Disclosure Policy

Aegis Cyber Defense values the security community and recognizes the importance of responsible disclosure. If you discover a security vulnerability in our systems or services, we encourage you to report it to us responsibly.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please email ceoeddieo@gmail.com with:
- A description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Suggested remediation (if any)

## What to Expect

- We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
- We will provide an initial assessment within 5 business days
- We will keep you updated on our progress
- We will coordinate public disclosure if appropriate

## Scope

Please only test systems that you are authorized to test. Do not:
- Access or modify data that does not belong to you
- Disrupt our services or systems
- Use automated vulnerability scanners
- Perform social engineering attacks

Thank you for helping keep Aegis Cyber Defense and our clients secure.
